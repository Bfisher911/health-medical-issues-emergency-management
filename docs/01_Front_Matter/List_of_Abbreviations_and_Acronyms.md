# List of Abbreviations and Acronyms

*The Health and Medical Issues of Emergency Management*

---

The following abbreviations and acronyms appear throughout this textbook. Definitions are provided at first use within each chapter; this list serves as a consolidated reference. Terms are organized alphabetically. Where an acronym has multiple meanings in different contexts, the primary usage in emergency management and public health is listed first.
